Method/Recipe ofIce Cream
  1. Heat gently, stirring, until smooth, then remove from the heat to cool slightly.
  2. Snap the chocolate into pieces, then place in a heavy-based saucepan with the milk.
  3. Beat the sugar and egg yolks in a bowl until pale and thick. Stir in the cooled chocolate milk, then strain back into the pan.
  4. Cook the custard over a gentle heat, stirring all the time, until it thickens and coats the back of a wooden spoon. Don’t allow the mixture boil or it will curdle.
  5. Pour into a bowl and leave to cool, stirring occasionally.
  6. Once cooled, whip the cream into soft peak
  7. and fold through the chocolate mixture.
  8. Churn in an ice cream machine, according to the manufacturer’s instructions, until it’s frozen.
Method/Recipe of Burger
  1. In large bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in water;
  2. let stand for 5 minutes. Add oil and salt. Stir in flour, 1 cup at a time, until a soft dough forms.
  3. Turn onto a floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, 2-3 minutes.
  4. Place in a greased bowl, turning once to grease the top.
  5. Cover and let rise in a warm place until doubled, about 45 minutes. Meanwhile, cook beef and onion over medium heat until beef is no longer pink, breaking meat into crumbles; drain.
  6. Punch down dough; divide in half. Press each half into a greased 12-in. pizza pan.
  7. the tomato sauce, oregano and basil; spread over each crust. Top with beef mixture, green pepper and cheese.
  8. Bake at 400° for 25-30 minutes or until crust is lightly browned.
